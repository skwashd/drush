Fixes #drushmageddon.

Download drush 6.7.0-dev

Drush relies on PEAR. PEAR has been down for 3 days. This means all automated builds that rely on a fresh version of drush 6 are failing. When managing and deploying Drupal at scale it isn't possible to just switch to a new version of drush or refactor your build scripts so they clone from HEAD. This site is a temporary work around until drush 6.7 is released.
